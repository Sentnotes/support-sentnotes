Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen. You'll receive an email with reset instructions.

How do I sync my health data?

Go to Settings → Health Integration and enable Apple HealthKit sync. Make sure you grant the necessary permissions.

How do I join a challenge?

Navigate to the Challenges section, browse available challenges, and tap "Join" on any challenge you'd like to participate in.

How do I contact my buddies?

Go to the Connections tab, select a buddy, and tap the message icon to start a conversation.
