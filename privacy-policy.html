SentNotes Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 4, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") explains how SentNotes ("SentNotes," "the App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, protects, and shares your information when you use our mobile application and related online services ("the Services"). The data controller for all personal information processed by the App is SentNotes.

By using the Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described herein.

Active Development Notice

SentNotes is currently in active development. As we continue to develop the app, certain features may change, improve, or evolve over time. Some data processing practices may also be refined as new features are introduced. If any changes materially affect how we collect or handle your data, we will update this Privacy Policy and notify you where appropriate.

Information We Collect

We collect information in several ways: directly from you, automatically through your device, and through optional third-party integrations. We collect only what is needed to operate SentNotes and provide useful insights, account features, and stability.

1. Information You Provide Directly

Account Information:

  • Name, email address, password (encrypted), username
  • Age (required, collected as integer during account creation)
  • Gender (required during account creation)
  • Profile picture (required during account creation)
  • Time zone (automatically detected from your device)
  • Subscription status and payment information
  • Bio, description, and other profile information (optional)

Wellness and Health Data:

  • Workout logs, exercise activities, and fitness goals
  • Activity tracking data (running, walking, cycling, swimming, etc.)
  • Mood tracking entries and journal notes
  • Meal logs, nutrition data, and dietary information
  • Saved meals (favorite meals saved for quick reuse)
  • Sleep tracking data (if manually entered or synced from HealthKit)
  • Health goals and progress tracking
  • Habit tracking and wellness entries
  • Weekly wellness summaries and progress reports
  • Challenge participation and progress
  • Achievement data

Social and Community Content:

  • Messages sent through in-app chat and messaging features
  • Posts, comments, and interactions within communities
  • Connection requests and relationship status with other users
  • Community memberships and participation
  • Challenge participation and leaderboard data

User-Generated Content:

  • Photos uploaded to your profile, posts, or shared with connections
  • Challenge images uploaded when creating challenges
  • Food photos taken for AI meal recognition
  • Files uploaded to your personal file storage
  • Saved meals data
  • Feedback, support messages, and communications

2. Information Collected Automatically

Device Information:

  • Device model, manufacturer, and operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App version and build number
  • Device language and region settings

Usage and Analytics Data:

  • Feature usage patterns and interaction data
  • Session duration, timestamps, and navigation patterns
  • Pages viewed and features accessed
  • Performance metrics and app responsiveness

Technical and Diagnostic Information:

  • Crash logs, error reports, and diagnostic data
  • Network information (connection type, IP address)
  • App performance metrics and stability data

3. Information From Third-Party Integrations

Apple HealthKit Integration (iOS):

If you choose to connect Apple HealthKit, SentNotes may request access to wellness and fitness data types. All data access requires your explicit permission for each category, and you can grant or deny access to individual data types.

Data types may include:

  • Activity & Movement: Steps, distance, workouts, active energy
  • Body Measurements: Height, weight, BMI
  • Heart Rate & Cardiovascular: Resting heart rate, heart rate variability
  • Sleep & Recovery: Sleep analysis, bedtime, wake time
  • Nutrition & Hydration: Calories, protein, carbs, vitamins, minerals
  • Mindfulness & Mental Wellness: Mindful session minutes

Important: All HealthKit data is used exclusively for wellness and fitness tracking. This data is NOT used for medical diagnosis, treatment, or clinical decision-making. SentNotes does NOT access Clinical Health Records API or medical records from healthcare providers. You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time through iOS Settings.

Other Third-Party Services:

  • Payment Processors: Payment processing data (processed securely)
  • Push Notification Services: Device tokens for push notification delivery
  • AI Service Providers: For nutrition advisor, food recognition, and wellness summaries

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to operate, improve, and personalize SentNotes:

Core Functionality:

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Authenticating your identity
  • Syncing your data across devices
  • Delivering personalized content and insights
  • Enabling social features (messaging, connections, communities)
  • Providing AI-powered features

We never use your wellness information or health data for advertising purposes. We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

How We Share Information

SentNotes does not sell your personal data. We only share data in limited circumstances:

Service Providers: We share information with trusted third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, subject to strict confidentiality agreements. These include cloud hosting, payment processing, push notifications, analytics, and AI services.

Third-Party Integrations You Enable: When you choose to connect third-party services (such as Apple HealthKit), data sharing occurs only as necessary to support the connection you activate.

Social Features: When you connect with other users, they can see your profile information as you have configured in your privacy settings. Messages you send are visible only to the intended recipient(s).

Legal and Safety Obligations: We may disclose information when legally required or to comply with applicable laws, respond to valid subpoenas, protect rights and safety, or prevent fraud.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary:

  • Account Data: While your account is active
  • Health and Wellness Data: Until deleted by you or upon account deletion
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years to comply with financial regulations

You may request deletion of your account and personal information at any time through App Settings or by emailing support@sentnotes.com.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Right to Correction: Correct inaccurate information
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw previously given consent
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your information

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@sentnotes.com with "Privacy Request" in the subject line.

Privacy Settings and Profile Visibility

You can control who can see your profile and activity information through privacy settings in the app:

  • Profile Visibility: This is your master privacy setting. You can set your profile to "Public" (visible to everyone), "Buddies Only" (visible only to your connections), or "Private" (visible only to you).
  • Achievements and Badges: The visibility of your achievements and badges automatically follows your Profile Visibility setting. If your profile is private, achievements and badges are also private.
  • Activity Visibility: When your Profile Visibility is set to "Private" or "Buddies Only", your activity visibility is automatically restricted to match. This prevents contradictory settings where your profile is private but activities are public.
  • Route Maps: Similar to activity visibility, route maps visibility is automatically restricted when your profile is set to "Private" or "Buddies Only".

You can adjust these settings at any time in Settings → Profile → Privacy Settings. Changes take effect immediately.

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest

However, no system is perfectly secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized activity.

SentNotes uses cloud infrastructure that may process data in various countries. When we transfer your personal information across borders, we implement appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), Data Processing Agreements, and encryption.
