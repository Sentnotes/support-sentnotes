Last updated: October 10, 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how SentNotes Kft. ("SentNotes", "we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your personal information when you use the SentNotes mobile application, website, and related tools (collectively, the "Services").

Although the application may currently be published under an individual Apple Developer account, SentNotes Kft. is the data controller and the sole entity responsible for processing your data under this Privacy Policy.

You may use the Services only if you agree with this Privacy Policy.

B. Personalization & Insights

We process your personal data under the following lawful bases:

6. How We Share Information

SentNotes does not sell personal data.

We share data only under these conditions:

A. Service Providers (Processors)

We use trusted third-party providers for:

  • Cloud hosting
  • Database management
  • Analytics
  • Customer support tools
  • Payment processors (Apple)

All processors follow strict confidentiality and security obligations.

B. Third-Party Integrations You Enable

For example: Apple Health, Google Fit

We only share data required to enable the integration and only with your consent.

C. Business Transfers

If SentNotes Kft. merges, is acquired, or restructures, your data may be transferred to the successor entity under the same protection terms.

D. Legal & Safety Requirements

We may disclose information if required by applicable law, court orders, government authorities, or to prevent harm, fraud, or security incidents.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in regions outside your home country. When this occurs, SentNotes Kft. applies safeguards such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Data Processing Agreements (DPAs)
  • Encryption during transit and storage
  • Minimization and purpose limitations

8. Data Retention

We retain:

  • Account data: until you delete your account
  • Wellness data: until deleted by you (or upon account deletion)
  • Analytics logs: typically 12–24 months
  • Backups: for limited periods before secure deletion

You may request deletion of all personal data at any time.

We implement industry-standard safeguards, including:
